Welcome to John Nursery & Primary Schools

John Nursery and Primary Schools are Universal English Medium Schools, providing excellent education and building global capacity to advance to higher levels of education. Located in Dar es Salaam, we enroll nursery (ages 2-6) and primary (grades 1-7) students.

Why Choose Us?

  • Competent management, teachers, and staff
  • Health care provided by a stationed nurse
  • Security through integrated CCTV systems
  • Conducive environment for the disabled
