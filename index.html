Welcome to Jubilation Nursery & Primary Schools

Jubilation Nursery and Primary Schools are Universal English Medium Schools, providing excellent education and building global capacity to advance to higher levels of education. Located in Dar es Salaam, we enroll nursery (ages 2-6) and primary (grades 1-7) students.

Why Choose Us?

  • Competent management, teachers, and staff
  • Health care provided by a stationed nurse
  • Security through integrated CCTV systems
  • Conducive environment for the disabled

About Us

Our Vision

To become the best pre & primary schools that provide well-educated, self-independent, and God-fearing young children who will make a positive impact on the communities and institutions they join after graduating from John Nursery & Primary Schools.

Our Mission

We aim to provide quality education, care, and a safe learning environment through our well-designed facilities and competent teaching staff, working together with compassion and teamwork to ensure our mission is accomplished.

Our Values

  • Quality: We maintain high standards through continuous improvement.
  • Innovation: We welcome change and encourage creative development.
  • Teamwork: We collaborate to achieve the best outcomes for our students.
  • Integrity: We adhere to high ethical and professional standards.
  • Compassion: We create a caring and supportive environment for everyone.

What We Offer

Early Childhood and Primary Education

We offer a combination of Ministry of Education curriculum and Montessori theories to provide a holistic approach to learning.

Transport Services

We provide a comfortable transport system with our fleet of school buses that cater to students in various locations.

Healthy Menu

We offer an interchangeable menu that ensures students get balanced, nutritious meals every day.
